Games have become too serious and one dimensional.

It’s all about graphics or expansive areas or first person shooters or extracting money from users.

It makes me think back to the launch of the Nintendo Wii into a very competitive landscape including the PS3
and XBOX 2. Yet it outsold every other console. It did this by creating games that could be shared by multiple
people with new paradigms.

I feel like the game industry needs another nintendo wii.
