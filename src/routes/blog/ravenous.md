He bristled. Explosive jolts of energy darted through his body reaching his extremities. His toes and fingers
twitched and curled and the pained expression on his face betrayed his desperate fight for control. Something
inhumane hade been awakened. Something deeply evil.

He knew this feeling all too well. The fiery embers has ignited within him and it was already too late to
stop, as his whole being, mind and body convulsed and shuddered in a cold and forceful way.

In desperation he tried to speak out, but by now his usually soft and calming voice was a raspy and terrifying
growl. His once kind eyes had been replaced with a vacant and blood freezing stare.

Thoughts once coherent and logical now coalesced on his target while the hunger he had suppressed for so long
boiled in his bones. It could no longer be contained. His mind reeled, fighting desperately for control. His
hands clasped at his face. Clawing. Scraping. Pulling his head down towards his chest, his mouth contorted,
howling in a piercing and unabated anguish.

Then, a thick and suffocating silence, more deafening than the ghoulish screams enveloped his surroundings.

For a moment his face remained shrouded in a fog of ambiguity, and hidden from plain sight it was impossible
to discern what or who had triumphed. Until, in one impossibly quick motion his head snapped upright. And even
without seeing him, a bystander would have been paralyzed by the cold waves of terror pulsating through his
being.

His nostrils flared.

His senses were now alert, alive to every single detail. He could smell the blood. He could taste the fear.
The savagery of his desire caused his breathing to quicken, his tongue salivated, and his throat choked in
anticipation of the pleasures to come.

Growling, and snarling he stumbled forward towards the target.

All notions of humanity were long gone.

The savage beast had returned.

And it was determined to savour every moment of this feast
